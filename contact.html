Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I get started?

Simply fill out the contact form or call us. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your requirements and propose the best solution.

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Small projects typically take 4-8 weeks, while enterprise solutions may take 3-6 months.

Do you provide support after project completion?

Yes, we provide 24/7 support and maintenance services. We can discuss support packages during your consultation.

What industries do you serve?

We serve multiple industries including finance, healthcare, retail, manufacturing, energy, and government sectors.

Can you work with our existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in system integration and can work with your existing infrastructure and legacy systems.

What about data security and compliance?

We maintain ISO 27001 and SOC 2 certifications and comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and other relevant compliance standards.
